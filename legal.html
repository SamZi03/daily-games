About This Site

Daily Streaks is a collection of free daily word and music games. This page explains how data is handled and credits the third-party services that make the games work.

Data We Collect

Daily Streaks does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game progress and streak data is saved locally in your browser using localStorage and never leaves your device.

Advertising

This site displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show you personalised ads based on your browsing activity across sites.

  • To learn how Google uses data when you use this site, visit Google's Privacy & Terms.
  • To opt out of personalised advertising, visit Google Ad Settings or aboutads.info.

Cookies

Daily Streaks itself does not set any cookies. Third-party advertising (Google AdSense) may set cookies on your device. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Third-Party Attributions

Music data & audio previews
Song metadata and audio previews are sourced from the Apple iTunes Search API. Use of this data is subject to Apple's iTunes Store Terms. Daily Streaks is not affiliated with or endorsed by Apple Inc.

Album artwork
Album artwork displayed on this site is the property of the respective record labels and artists. Artwork is retrieved via the iTunes Search API solely for the purpose of gameplay and is not reproduced for commercial distribution.

Stream count data
Spotify stream figures used in StatLock are approximate and sourced from publicly available information. Daily Streaks is not affiliated with Spotify.
